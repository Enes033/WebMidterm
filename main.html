As the name implies, the place has a strong connection with art. Atelier means a room or studio where creativity and art are in focus, offered here by us in the form of food, drinks, atmosphere and good service. It is located in the middle of the city's busiest and historical street in Shkodër. As you enter the wooden gate you will be welcomed with a beautiful garden. The first stones in the building were laid in the late 18th century and it served as the house of several important local families. Today we are introducing it as a restaurant and boutique hotel. The stone walls testify to the history of our city, create the proper privacy for our guests. While eating “al fresco” under the light of summer stars, reminiscent of the beautiful Mediterranean climate and culture.
